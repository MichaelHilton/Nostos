Mark Anatomy

Spiral Wave
An Archimedean spiral open on one side — suggesting an Aegean wave and an incomplete orbit, evoking a journey not yet finished.
Lens Circle
The outer ring and central aperture reference a camera lens — the act of capturing and preserving memory.
Gold North Point
A compass N-mark in gold — the needle pointing home, for nostos, the Greek word for homecoming.
Minimum Size
The mark holds down to 16px. Below 20px, use a simplified version without the outer ring and inner aperture circle.
Clear Space
Maintain clear space equal to the radius of the outer ring (≈ 37% of mark width) on all sides.
Don't Do
Don't rotate the mark. Don't change the gold north-point to any other color. Don't place on non-brand backgrounds without checking contrast.
